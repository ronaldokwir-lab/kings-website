About Us

The OBS & OGS Bishop Kami Nursery & Primary School Alumni Association is a vibrant community dedicated to fostering connections among former students. Established with the vision of creating a lifelong network, the association aims to bring together alumni from various backgrounds and experiences to share their journeys, successes, and memories.

Our mission is to strengthen the bonds formed during formative years at Bishop Kami, providing a platform for alumni to engage in meaningful discussions, mentorship, and collaboration. We believe in the power of community, and through our association, we strive to support current students and the school by giving back through initiatives such as scholarships, community service, and educational programs.

As members of this esteemed association, we cherish the values instilled in us by our beloved schoolâ€”integrity, respect, and a commitment to excellence. We invite all former students to join us in celebrating our shared history, contributing to our legacy, and inspiring future generations.

Together, we can make a positive impact on our communities and continue the spirit of Bishop Kami Nursery & Primary School long after our time in the classroom. Join us as we connect, inspire, and empower each other in our journeys beyond the school walls.
